ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm excited to share a little about myself with you. I am a recent graduate and I majored in Business Administration at Pepperdine University located in Malibu, CA. Overall I'm very excited to see where this journey takes me and am eager to continue exploring the vast possibilities that coding has to offer.
